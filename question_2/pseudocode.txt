
Input
List of doctors 
List of patients

Process
1. Create a list of doctors’ gender, specialization, ID, and their available time.
2. Create a list of patients' preferred gender and their specialized needs.
3. Loop through the patient’s hashmap
4. If the specialized needs and the gender preference of the patient match the gender and the specialization of the doctor, allocate the available time of the doctor to the patient.
5. Set a reminder for the patient.
6. Send a reminder to the patient 24 hours before.


Output
String.
